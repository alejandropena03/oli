05 — Usage Guidelines

Do / Don't

✓ DO

Use the wordmark in all-lowercase. Always.

✗ DON'T

Never write "/Oli", "/OLI", or "Oli". Lowercase is the identity.

✓ DO

Keep the "/" in cyan (#00E5FF) on dark, or cyan-dim (#00B8CC) on light backgrounds.

✗ DON'T

Never use the "/" in white, black, or any non-cyan color. It loses brand meaning.

✓ DO

Use terminal green (#00FF87) exclusively for success / completion states.

✗ DON'T

Never use green as a primary brand color or decorative element. It means "done."

✓ DO

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "/" character.

✗ DON'T

Never place the logo on busy backgrounds, gradient noise, or colorful photography without a dark overlay.

07 — Recommended Direction

Final Call

Primary Mark: Route B (Slash Command) + Route E (Industrial Circle)

Route B is the strongest icon candidate. The geometric slash is bold, distinctive, and conceptually perfect: /oli is a command, not a chatbot. It works at all sizes, renders cleanly in app icons and favicons, and is unlike anything in the SaaS landscape.

Route E is the premium wordmark companion. The circle-slash mark gives /oli a precision-instrument quality — like a certified operator mark. Use Route E for the full logo lockup in press kits, partnerships, and premium placements.

System architecture: Route B icon for app icon, favicon, and social profile. Route E full mark for website header and official documents. Route A pure wordmark for inline text references. Route C and D as decorative/textural elements in backgrounds and pattern work.
